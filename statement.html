Artist Statement

For this project, I wanted to create something that would give everyone a boost of serotonin. It’s finals, everyone is stressed, and what better way to destress than to see a bunch of cute ducks in a cute forest. One of my favorite games to play is Animal Crossing and it’s the game I play to relax and unwind so I got my main inspiration from there. I used three.js, GLTFLoader.js, and PointerLockControls.js.
